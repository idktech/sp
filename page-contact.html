How to get to our head office:

Getting here by tube

Aldgate East tube station is 3 minutes walk form our head office and is the nearest one. Yo can get District line, Hammersmith & City line or Circle line Plan your journey at TFL.

Getting here by Car

Park in designated parking bays on Commercial road indicated by signs showing the P (Parking) symbol. White bays: If a red route parking bay is marked out by a white dotted line, it generally means that the bay can be used throughout the day. Red bays: it indicates that the bay can be used outside peak traffic hours, however, peak traffic hours often vary from area to area and even from road to road. Always check the signs. There may be a maximum time limit - always check the signs.
